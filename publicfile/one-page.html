Are You a Startup or Student?


Why Join?


Start Connect's vision is to facilitate productivity and expand the capabilities for startups while providing students with the opportunity to develop and exercise valued skills.
We connect students with local startups that have available volunteer internship positions. We work alongside startups to ensure the the best service can be attained.
Gain valuable experience for yourself or for your company with Start Connect.



Grow Your Network


Have an opportunity to connect with hundreds of other founders and entrepreneurs. Network is your biggest asset!

Hands on Learning


Apply your skillsets and learn new things. Take part in building a startup and see your work make a difference.

Flexible Hours


Create a schedule that fits you whether it be 10 hours or 40 hours, so you can study full time if you want!

Building Resume


Build your resume to stack up on the experiences you gained from the internship to land your dream jobs.

Reference Letters


Get reference letters from the founders to give yourself a competitive advantage from other applicants for future employment.

Future Placements


By interning and getting familiar with the startups, have return opportunities for your future after startups grow.

The Team


The team consists of highly entrepreneurial mathematics students from the University of Waterloo. They came together with a common goal of solving unemployment among students. Getting your first several co-ops is hard and it is also hard to get the job you want. It is also painful paying hundreds of dollars to get co-op each time. Start Connect hopes to solve these problems.
